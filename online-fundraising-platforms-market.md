<p><p>Online fundraising platforms operate primarily in two market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based platforms provide accessible, scalable solutions hosted on remote servers, allowing users to manage fundraising activities from anywhere with internet access. They often offer subscription-based pricing and regular updates.
