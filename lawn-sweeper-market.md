<p><p>The lawn sweeper market consists mainly of two types: push-along sweepers and tow-behind sweepers. Push-along sweepers are manually operated units ideal for smaller residential areas, allowing for easy maneuverability and user control. In contrast, tow-behind sweepers are designed for larger properties and can be attached to a lawn tractor or ATV, enabling efficient cleanup of extensive landscapes. Both types are essential for maintaining lawns by collecting debris, leaves, and grass clippings while enhancing overall yard appearance and health.</p></p>
