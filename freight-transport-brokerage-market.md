LTL focuses on smaller shipments consolidated with goods from multiple customers, allowing for economical transportation of less voluminous cargo. Other markets can include partial truckload or intermodal services, which combine different transportation modes, catering to diverse logistical needs and enhancing supply chain flexibility.</p></p>
