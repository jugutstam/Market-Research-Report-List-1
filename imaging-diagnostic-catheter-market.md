<p><p>The Imaging Diagnostic Catheter market encompasses various types of specialized catheters used for diagnostic procedures. Angiography catheters are employed to visualize blood vessels through contrast imaging. Ultrasound catheters utilize sound waves to produce real-time images of soft tissues. OCT catheters leverage optical coherence tomography for high-resolution imaging of vascular structures. Electrophysiology (EP) catheters assess electrical activity in the heart. Other imaging catheters may include specialized tools for specific diagnostic applications, catering to diverse medical needs.</p></p>
