<p><p>The Intelligent Buoy System market consists of three main types: Active Sonobuoys, Passive Sonobuoys, and Special Purpose Sonobuoys. Active Sonobuoys emit sound waves to detect underwater objects, enhancing tactical naval operations. Passive Sonobuoys, on the other hand, listen for sounds, such as submarines, providing crucial intelligence without revealing their position. Special Purpose Sonobuoys are designed for specific missions, including environmental monitoring or scientific research, offering tailored solutions that address various operational requirements in maritime environments.</p></p>
