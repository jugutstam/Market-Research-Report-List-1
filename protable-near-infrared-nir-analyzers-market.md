<p><p>Portable Near Infrared (NIR) analyzers are categorized into several types, primarily including FT-NIR (Fourier Transform Near Infrared) and AOTF-NIR (Acousto-Optic Tunable Filter NIR). FT-NIR analyzers utilize multiple wavelengths simultaneously for rapid analysis, making them suitable for complex samples. AOTF-NIR analyzers, in contrast, offer rapid wavelength tuning, allowing for precise measurements without moving parts. Other market types may encompass handheld or compact NIR devices tailored for specific applications, enhancing convenience and versatility in various fields like agriculture, food safety, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
