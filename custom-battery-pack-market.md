<p><p>The custom battery pack market encompasses several types, each with distinct characteristics. Lithium-Ion (Li-Ion) batteries are favored for their high energy density and lightweight properties, making them ideal for portable electronics. Lithium-Polymer (Li-Poly) batteries offer flexible shapes and improved safety. Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries provide a good balance of energy capacity and longevity. Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) batteries are known for their durability but suffer from memory effect. Sealed Lead Acid (SLA) batteries are cost-effective and reliable for backup power applications.</p></p>
