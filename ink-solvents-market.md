<p><p>The ink solvents market comprises various types, including alcohol, ester, benzene, and ketone solvents. Alcohol solvents, often used for their quick evaporation rates, enhance ink drying times. Ester solvents provide excellent solvency and are ideal for vibrant inks. Benzene solvents, though effective, face regulatory scrutiny due to health concerns. Ketone solvents are known for their strong solvent properties, commonly used in specialty inks. Each type plays a vital role in the formulation and performance of inks across diverse applications.</p></p>
