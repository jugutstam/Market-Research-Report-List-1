Completed feed provides a balanced diet and is ready for immediate use, containing all essential nutrients. Concentrated feed includes high-nutrient ingredients that require mixing with other feed components to create a complete diet. Premixed feed consists of a blend of vitamins, minerals, and supplements designed to be added to base feeds, enhancing nutritional value. Each type serves distinct needs in poultry farming.</p></p>
