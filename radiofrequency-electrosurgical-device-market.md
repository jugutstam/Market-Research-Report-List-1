<p><strong>The Radiofrequency Electrosurgical Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Devices</li><li>Bipolar Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiofrequency electrosurgical device market is primarily divided into monopolar and bipolar devices. Monopolar devices utilize a single electrode to cut or coagulate tissue, relying on a grounding pad to complete the circuit, making them ideal for deep tissue procedures. In contrast, bipolar devices use two electrodes, allowing current to flow between them, which offers more precise control over tissue effects and minimizes collateral damage. Both types are widely used in various surgical applications, enhancing surgical precision and patient safety.</p></p>
