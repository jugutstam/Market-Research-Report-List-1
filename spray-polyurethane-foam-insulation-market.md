<p><p>The Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) insulation market is primarily divided into two types: open-cell foam (ocSPF) and closed-cell foam (ccSPF). Open-cell foam is lighter and more flexible, allowing for excellent sound absorption and moisture barrier properties, making it ideal for interior applications. In contrast, closed-cell foam is denser and offers superior thermal insulation and structural integrity, suitable for exterior applications and areas exposed to moisture. Each type serves distinct insulation needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
