Video Baby Monitors provide real-time video feeds, enabling parents to visually monitor their infant's activities. Internet Baby Monitors connect to Wi-Fi, allowing remote access via smartphones or tablets, offering features like notifications and cloud storage. These advancements enhance parental assurance and convenience in monitoring their child's safety.</p></p>
