<p><p>The Laser Annealer market is primarily segmented into two types: Line Beam Type and Multi Lens Array Type. Line Beam Type uses a single, high-intensity laser beam that travels in a straight line, optimizing energy distribution for uniform heating across larger areas. In contrast, Multi Lens Array Type utilizes multiple lenses to focus several laser beams simultaneously, allowing for precise annealing of smaller regions and enhancing efficiency. Both types cater to diverse applications in semiconductor manufacturing and material processing.</p></p>
