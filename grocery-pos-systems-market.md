<p><p>Grocery POS systems are classified into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based systems operate online, allowing real-time access and updates from various devices, which enhances flexibility and reduces hardware costs. They typically offer subscription models. On-premise systems, however, are installed directly on local servers and offer greater control and security, but require significant upfront investment in hardware and maintenance. Each type serves different business needs, with cloud solutions often favored by smaller grocery retailers for their scalability.</p></p>
