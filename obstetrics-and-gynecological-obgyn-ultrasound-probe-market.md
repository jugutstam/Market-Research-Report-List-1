<p><p>The OB/GYN ultrasound probe market features two primary types: ceramic probes and single crystal probes. Ceramic probes utilize a piezoelectric ceramic material, offering a balance of cost-effectiveness and imaging quality, making them suitable for routine examinations. In contrast, single crystal probes use advanced materials that enhance sensitivity and resolution, providing clearer images, particularly in complex cases. These technologies cater to different clinical needs, driving innovation and differentiation within the obstetrics and gynecology imaging field.</p></p>
