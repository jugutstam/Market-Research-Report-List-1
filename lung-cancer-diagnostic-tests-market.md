Tissue arrays enable simultaneous examination of multiple samples, while circulating tumor cells are identified from blood samples. Pharmacodiagnostics guide personalized treatment responses, and biomarkers serve as indicators of disease presence or progression. Other diagnostic methods include imaging techniques and biopsies.</p></p>
