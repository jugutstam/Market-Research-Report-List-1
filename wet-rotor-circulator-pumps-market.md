<p><p>Wet rotor circulator pumps are classified into two main types: vertical wet rotor circulator pumps and horizontal wet rotor circulator pumps. Vertical wet rotor pumps are designed to operate with the motor above the pump, allowing for compact installation and efficient use of space, particularly in heating and cooling systems. Horizontal wet rotor pumps have a more traditional layout, often providing higher flow rates and easier maintenance. Both types are utilized in various applications, including HVAC systems, solar energy, and domestic hot water circulation.</p></p>
