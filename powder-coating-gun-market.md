<p><ul><li>Corona Powder Coating Gun</li><li>Tribo Powder Coating Gun</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powder coating gun market features two main types: Corona and Tribo powder coating guns. **Corona powder coating guns** utilize an electric field to charge powder particles, ensuring even coating on various surfaces. They are widely used due to their efficiency and effectiveness in large-scale applications. **Tribo powder coating guns**, on the other hand, rely on mechanical friction to charge the powder as it passes through the gun, offering versatility in coating uneven or complex shapes. Both types cater to distinct operational needs in the coating industry.</p></p>
