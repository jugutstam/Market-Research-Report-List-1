<p><p>The pipetting tip market comprises two main types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are designed for general use, providing a reliable seal and accurate volume transfer without any additional filtration. In contrast, filtered pipette tips contain a built-in filter that prevents contamination and aerosol formation, making them ideal for handling volatile or hazardous substances. Both types play crucial roles in laboratories, with choices depending on the specific applications and safety requirements of various experiments.</p></p>
