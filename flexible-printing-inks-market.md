<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible printing inks market encompasses primarily two types: water-based and solvent-based inks. Water-based inks utilize water as a solvent, making them environmentally friendly with lower VOC emissions, ideal for a variety of substrates. Solvent-based inks, on the other hand, use organic solvents, offering superior adhesion and durability on challenging materials but often at the cost of higher emissions. Both types cater to diverse applications in flexible packaging, labels, and various printing processes, influencing industry choices based on performance and sustainability.</p></p>
