<p><p>The confectionery wrappers market comprises various materials, each offering unique benefits. Cast polypropylene (CPP) is flexible and provides excellent moisture resistance, making it ideal for candy packaging. Polyethylene terephthalate (PET) is known for its clarity and strength, often used for high-quality visual appeal. Biaxially oriented polypropylene (BOPP) is durable, offers better barrier properties, and is commonly used for snacks. Polyethylene (PE) is versatile and cost-effective, frequently utilized in various packaging solutions for its sealing capabilities and flexibility.</p></p>
