<p><p>The positive electron beam resists market primarily includes types such as PMMA, PMMA/MA copolymer, and other formulations. PMMA (Poly(methyl methacrylate)) is widely used due to its excellent resolution and sensitivity in electron beam lithography. PMMA/MA copolymer combines PMMA with methacrylic acid to enhance the resist's performance, offering improved etching and thermal stability. Other types encompass various novel resists that aim to push the boundaries of resolution and processing speed, catering to advancements in nanotechnology and semiconductor applications.</p></p>
