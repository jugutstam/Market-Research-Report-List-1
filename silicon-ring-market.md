Medium resistivity silicon rings balance conductivity and thermal performance, making them suitable for various electronic applications. High resistivity silicon rings exhibit low conductivity, ideal for high-frequency and RF applications where minimal signal loss is critical, offering enhanced isolation and reduced crosstalk in circuits.</p></p>
