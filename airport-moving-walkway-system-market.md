<p><p>The Airport Moving Walkway System market comprises two main types: belt type and pallet type. Belt type systems utilize a continuous belt to transport passengers smoothly, ideal for longer distances with a focus on comfort. In contrast, pallet type systems feature a flat, moving surface, resembling escalators, designed for quicker passenger flow with minimal incline. Both systems enhance airport efficiency, reducing transit times and improving passenger convenience, catering to varied airport layouts and passenger volumes.</p></p>
