<p><ul><li>Non-Filtered Pipette Tips</li><li>Filtered Pipette Tips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipette tips market can be categorized into non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are designed for general liquid transfer applications, providing accurate measurement without additional filtration. In contrast, filtered pipette tips feature a barrier that prevents aerosols and contaminants from entering the pipette, enhancing sample integrity and minimizing cross-contamination risks. This distinction is crucial for laboratories requiring stringent contamination control, such as in molecular biology and clinical diagnostics, ensuring reliable and consistent results across various applications.</p></p>
