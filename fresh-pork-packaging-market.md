<p><strong>The Fresh Pork Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP)</li><li>Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP)</li><li>Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fresh Pork Packaging market features several key types. Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) replaces the air inside the package with a specific gas mix, extending shelf life. Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) tightly wraps the product in a film that forms a vacuum seal, enhancing appearance and freshness. Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP) involves molding plastic around the product under vacuum conditions, ensuring protection and extended storage. Other methods include traditional vacuum packaging and various protective films to maintain quality and freshness.</p></p>
