<p><p>The Microwave Moisture Sensor market comprises two key types: Volumetric Moisture Sensors and Water Potential Moisture Sensors. Volumetric Moisture Sensors measure the water content within a sample by analyzing the dielectric properties of materials, providing an estimate of moisture levels based on volume. In contrast, Water Potential Moisture Sensors assess the moisture availability to plants by evaluating the tension or pressure within the water in soil or substrates. Both types are crucial in agriculture and construction for optimizing moisture management.</p></p>
