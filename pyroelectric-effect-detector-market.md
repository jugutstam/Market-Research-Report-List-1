<p><p>The Pyroelectric Effect Detector market comprises various types, each utilizing different mechanisms for thermal detection. The thermoelectric type converts temperature differences into electrical voltage, providing reliable measurements. Thermistor type detectors rely on resistance changes with temperature, offering high sensitivity. Pneumatic detectors utilize gas expansion due to temperature changes, resulting in mechanical action for detection. Lastly, pyroelectric type detectors generate voltage in response to temperature fluctuations, making them effective for infrared sensing applications. Each type serves distinct thermal monitoring needs.</p></p>
