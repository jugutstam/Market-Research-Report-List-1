<p><p>The ultrasonic cleaning solution market is categorized into three main types: alkaline, acidic, and neutral solutions. Alkaline solutions are effective in removing organic contaminants such as grease and oils due to their high pH level. Acidic solutions target mineral deposits, rust, and oxidation, making them suitable for metal surfaces. Neutral solutions offer a balanced pH, making them versatile for cleaning various materials without risk of damage. Each type serves distinct cleaning purposes based on the nature of contaminants and substrates.</p></p>
