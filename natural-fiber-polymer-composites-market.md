Woven composites involve interlacing fibers, providing enhanced strength and durability, making them suitable for structural applications. Specialty forms include unique designs like reinforced biocomposites or those with specific functionalities, catering to niche markets such as medical devices and high-performance products, thus expanding the versatility and applicability of natural fiber composites.</p></p>
