<p><p>Molecular imaging encompasses various advanced imaging techniques that visualize biological processes at the molecular level. The PET (Positron Emission Tomography) market focuses on metabolic imaging, often combined with CT (Computed Tomography) for PET-CT, enhancing anatomical accuracy. PET-MR merges PET with Magnetic Resonance Imaging for superior soft tissue contrast. SPECT (Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography) provides functional imaging using gamma cameras. MR spectroscopy analyzes metabolic profiles, while molecular ultrasound imaging leverages ultrasound technology to visualize molecular interactions, amplifying diagnostic capabilities across fields.</p></p>
