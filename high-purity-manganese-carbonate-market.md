<p><p>High purity manganese carbonate is categorized into three market grades based on purity levels: 3N, 4N, and 5N. The 3N grade contains 99.9% manganese, often used in battery production and ceramics. The 4N grade, with 99.99% purity, caters to applications requiring higher quality, such as advanced electronics and specialty glass. The 5N grade, which is 99.999% pure, is essential for demanding applications in pharmaceuticals and high-performance batteries, where even trace impurities can affect functionality.</p></p>
