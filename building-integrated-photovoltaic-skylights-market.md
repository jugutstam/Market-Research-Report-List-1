<p><p>Building Integrated Photovoltaic (BIPV) skylights incorporate solar panels directly into building structures, offering aesthetic appeal and energy generation. The market primarily comprises two types: crystalline panels and thin-film panels. Crystalline panels are efficient and widely used, featuring high power output and durability. In contrast, thin-film panels are lightweight and flexible, allowing for easier integration into various architectural designs, although they generally have lower efficiency. Both types contribute to sustainable energy solutions while enhancing natural lighting in buildings.</p></p>
