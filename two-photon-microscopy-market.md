<p><p>Two-Photon Microscopy is an advanced imaging technique that utilizes the simultaneous absorption of two photons to excite fluorescent molecules, allowing for high-resolution imaging of biological tissues. This method is particularly effective for deep tissue imaging, as it minimizes photodamage and enhances penetration depth compared to traditional microscopy techniques.
