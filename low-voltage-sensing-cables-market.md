<p><p>Low voltage sensing cables utilize fiber optics to monitor and detect environmental changes or conditions. The market is divided into single-mode and multi-mode fiber types. Single-mode fiber offers higher bandwidth and longer transmission distances, ideal for long-range applications. In contrast, multi-mode fiber supports shorter distances with multiple light paths, making it suitable for data centers and shorter networks. Both types are essential in various industries for reliable sensing applications, enhancing safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
