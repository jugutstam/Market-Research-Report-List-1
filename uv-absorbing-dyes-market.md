<p><p>The UV absorbing dyes market can be divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based formulations. Water-based dyes are environmentally friendly, offering easier application and clean-up, making them popular for textile and coating industries focused on sustainability. In contrast, solvent-based dyes provide enhanced durability and stronger color saturation, making them suitable for applications requiring resistance to fading and chemical exposure, such as industrial coatings and printing inks. Both types play vital roles in enhancing UV protection and color retention in various products.</p></p>
