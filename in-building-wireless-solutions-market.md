<p><p>In-building wireless solutions primarily consist of Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) and small cell networks. DAS utilize a network of antennas and digital signal boosters to enhance cellular coverage in large structures, ensuring seamless connectivity indoors. Small cell systems provide localized coverage and capacity, addressing high-demand areas like stadiums and shopping malls by using low-power nodes. Both technologies are essential for improving user experience, accommodating increasing mobile data traffic, and enhancing overall network performance in urban environments.</p></p>
