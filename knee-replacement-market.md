<p><p>The knee replacement market can be categorized into three main types: Primary Knee Replacement, Partial Knee Replacement, and Revision Knee Replacement. Primary Knee Replacement involves the full replacement of a damaged knee joint, commonly due to arthritis. Partial Knee Replacement targets only the affected section of the knee, preserving healthy tissue. Revision Knee Replacement is a surgical procedure to replace or repair an already implanted prosthesis that has failed or deteriorated, ensuring improved joint function and relief from pain.</p></p>
