<p><p>Fiber Grade Polylactic Acid (PLA) is an environmentally friendly biopolymer used in textile applications. The market is segmented into two primary types: monofilament and multifilament. Monofilament PLA consists of single, continuous fibers that offer strength and durability, ideal for applications like fishing lines and dental floss. Multifilament PLA, on the other hand, comprises multiple intertwined fibers, resulting in a softer texture, suitable for clothing and industrial fabrics. Both types provide sustainable alternatives in various industries, promoting eco-friendly practices.</p></p>
