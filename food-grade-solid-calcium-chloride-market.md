<p><ul><li>Calcium Chloride Dihydrate</li><li>Calcium Chloride Anhydrous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food grade solid calcium chloride market is primarily categorized into two types: calcium chloride dihydrate and calcium chloride anhydrous. Calcium chloride dihydrate contains two water molecules, making it suitable for applications requiring moderate moisture levels, such as food preservation and refrigeration. In contrast, calcium chloride anhydrous, which lacks water, is highly effective as a drying agent and is often used in food processing and packaging. Both types are essential for enhancing food safety and quality.</p></p>
